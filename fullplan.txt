The 10-Step Execution Plan
Here is your blueprint. We will build the backend completely first, test it, and then build the frontend on top of it.

Phase 1: Backend (Node.js, Express, MongoDB)
Step 1: Foundation & Database Models

What I will give you: server.js, config/db.js, and the 4 Mongoose Schemas (User.js, Listing.js, Message.js, Rating.js). This sets up your database structure.

Step 2: Authentication System

What I will give you: authController.js, authRoutes.js, and authMiddleware.js. This handles secure registration (enforcing college emails) and login using JWT.

Step 3: Core Listings & Local Image Uploads

What I will give you: uploadMiddleware.js (Multer), listingController.js (Create, Read, Update, Delete), and listingRoutes.js. This allows the app to accept textbook data and save images locally.

Step 4: Advanced Search, Wishlist & Ratings

What I will give you: Complex search logic in listingController.js (regex, price filters), plus userController.js and userRoutes.js for adding books to the Wishlist and leaving Ratings.

Step 5: Admin Panel & Real-Time Messaging

What I will give you: adminMiddleware.js, adminController.js, adminRoutes.js (for analytics and moderation), plus chatSocket.js, messageController.js, and messageRoutes.js for Socket.io chat functionality.

Phase 2: Frontend (React.js, Tailwind CSS)
Step 6: Frontend Scaffold & Global State

What I will give you: App.jsx (Routing), main.jsx, Tailwind configuration, and the AuthContext.jsx to manage the logged-in user across the app.

Step 7: Auth UI & Public Navigation: ThemeToggle.jsx, Navbar.jsx, Login.jsx, and Register.jsx. We will use the light/airy design here.

Step 8: The Bento-Box Scaffold & Discovery: Sidebar.jsx, DashboardLayout.jsx, Home.jsx, and ListingCard.jsx. We switch to the dark, glassmorphic grid here.

Step 9: Listing Interactions & Creation: CreateListing.jsx and BookDetails.jsx.

Step 10: The User Ecosystem: ChatWindow.jsx, Wishlist.jsx, Profile.jsx, and AdminDashboard.jsx.
